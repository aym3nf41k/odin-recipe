Burgers

Description

Burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

A burger patty topped with cheese is called a cheeseburger.

Under some definitions, and in some cultures, a burger is considered a sandwich.

Burgers are typically associated with fast-food restaurants and diners but are also sold at various other restaurants, including more expensive high-end establishments.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Bread crumbs
  • Lettuce
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare Burger

  1. Grill the beef patty until cooked.
  2. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  3. Place the patty on the bottom bun.
  4. Add a slice of cheese on top of the patty.
  5. Layer lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles.
  6. Spread ketchup and mayonnaise on the top bun.
  7. Close the burger and enjoy!
